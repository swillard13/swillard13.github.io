About

1-800-EAT-CAKE is a small internet business founded by four students at the University of Southern California in Spring 2017. We partner with local bakeries all across the country so customers can order from a bakery near them and get an artisan cake delivered fresh. We aim to fix problems presented but other cake delivery options such as lack of freshness and inability to customize. In the future, we plan to introduce more customization capability including lettering and simple designs, as well as expand into the pet-friendly treat and dessert markets. Our target customer is anyone who wants a convenient way to order a gourmet cake - Bon Appetit!
