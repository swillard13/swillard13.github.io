Cake Partners Update!

Hey everyone, I'm glad you're keeping up with our progress! We've made some important partnerships with some local bakeries to bring the best cakes to YOU! Here's the long list of all the bakeries we've currently gotten:

Fresh from the beautiful South LA area, you get La Favorita Bakery, a longtime favorite eatery of locals

Magnolia Bakery is one of our favorite bakeries in the Beverly Hills area. They consider homemade cakes on of their specialities!

Proof Bakery is a charming hole-in-the-wall located in Glendale capable of quickly filling that hole-in-your-stomach

Charm City Cakes is a vibrant and energetic cake shop boasting a fancy collection of upscale cakes and treats

SusieCakes, located in LA Brea, is an All-American bakery offering classic desserts made entirely from scratch using simple, natural ingredients

La Monarca Bakery's founders grew up enjoying the rich variety of cakes, pastries and breads which are household staples in Mexico. They created La Monarca Bakery to bring The Sweet Flavor of Mexico to the U.S.

Erin McKenna's Bakery located in Central LA is a quaint NYC import specializing in dairy-free, gluten-free cupcakes, cookies & other baked goods.

Each of these bakeries went through a rigorous selection process evaluating ingredient quality, dessert aesthetic, and business orderliness. Our partnerships will ensure high quality fresh dessert delivered in a timely fashion. If you have more bakeries you would like added to this list please contact us via our contact form. You can see an updated list of our partners by visiting Our Partner page! More updates to come, so stay tuned. -XOXO, Your Cake Team
